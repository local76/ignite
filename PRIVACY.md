rStartup is built with a commitment to user privacy. As a local terminal application, rStartup does not track, collect, or transmit your personal data.

Data Collection and Telemetry
rStartup does not include any analytics, crash reporting platforms, or tracking metrics. It does not collect or request any personal identifiers, email addresses, or system credentials.

Network Connections
rStartup runs completely offline and does not make any network connections.

Local Data Storage
All data managed by rStartup is kept strictly on your local machine under your user profile directory.
Configurations are stored in config.yaml under the rStartup folder in your AppData directory.
Logs are written to log.txt under the rStartup folder in your AppData directory.
rStartup does not upload your local configuration files or logs to any remote server.
